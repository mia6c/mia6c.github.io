Meet Our Team

Say hi! to our team

Satiated Saturday brings together a vibrant and diverse group of international students in Ho Chi Minh City, representing schools such as CIS, SSIS, and ISHMC. This dynamic organization is comprised of enthusiastic and culturally aware members, including Mia, Mary, Olivia, Yoonhae, Hermione, Ann, Surio, Aurora, and Willy. Each member brings their unique background, perspective, and talents to the group, fostering a rich tapestry of experiences and ideas.
